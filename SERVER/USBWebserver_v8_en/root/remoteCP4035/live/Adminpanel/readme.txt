Requirements:
-------------
The plugin needs at least remoteCP 4.0.3.2 up to 4.0.3.4
remoteCP 4.0.3.5 needs v1.5 or newer


Plugin:
--------
Displays a Adminpanel over the Chat durring the Race and the Score.
This Plugin shows in the Chat if a Admin (all who have the rights for "editmaps" and "editplayer")
	- restarted a challenge,
	- forced next challenge,
	- forced end of the round,
and	- canceled the vote;


Install:
--------
1. Copy all folders to your remoteCP-4 /live/ directory
2. Open your /xml/settings/default/live.xml and add inside <plugins></plugins>:

	<plugin>Adminpanel</plugin>

3. Restart your remoteCP4 Live and enjoy :)
